A Note on Backlinks

What is a Backlink?

A backlink is when another website places a link pointing to your website. Google treats this as a signal of credibility — the more reputable sites that reference you, the more Google trusts your site. Think of it as digital word-of-mouth.

Why Backlink Outreach is Not Included

Active backlink building involves reaching out to other website owners, writing guest articles, and building ongoing relationships with publishers. This is the most resource-intensive part of SEO — which is why agencies that include it charge RM 3,000–5,000+ per month. It is not suitable for a starter package.

What is included instead: As a practical first step, this plan includes a one-time submission of your business to reputable local directories (Google Business Profile, Yelp Malaysia, Hotfrog, FourSquare, Malaysia SME Directory). These count as foundational backlinks and are sufficient for a business at this stage of SEO. Active outreach can be explored as an add-on in the future should you wish to grow more aggressively.
